# HydroWild — Brand Reference

## Company
- Zero-sugar, zero-dye kids/teens hydration drink mix (stick packs)
- Founded 2022 by CJ Britton & Lindey Britton — Birmingham, AL
- Tagline: "get wild. stay hydrated"
- Positioning: clean, science-backed alternative to sugary sports drinks
- Current site: hydrowild.com (Shopify)

## Fonts
- Archivo (headings), Barlow (body) — both on Google Fonts

## Products ($14.99/box)
- Blue Raspberry, Watermelon, Strawberry Lemonade, Fruit Punch
- Wild Starter Kit, Wild Variety Pack (all 4 flavors)
- Shopify handles: kids-daily-hydration-drink-mix-{flavor}, wild-starter-kit, wild-variety-pack-all-4-flavors

## Key ingredients story
- Electrolytes: potassium, magnesium
- Vitamins: A, C, D, K, B6, B12, folate
- Sweetened with stevia. No sugar, no dyes.
